Agriculture is the art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops and raising livestock.

Aquaculture is breeding, raising, and harvesting fish, shellfish, and aquatic plants.

Agriculture is practiced for the purpose of producing food and other human needs such as clothing, shelter, medicines, weapons, tools, ornaments, and indefinitely many more including livestock feed.
